FAQ

What if I don’t know which fiction book to choose?

Our staff will happily help you find the perfect read. We’ll ask about your favorite genres and recommend books, even letting you sample a chapter with your tea.

Can I reserve a specific book every visit?

We don’t offer long-term reservations to ensure all guests can enjoy our collection. We can help you locate editions, suggest purchase options, or guide you to quieter times to find your favorites.

What if the café gets noisy while reading?

Teas & Tales is designed as a peaceful space. Staff will kindly remind guests to keep voices low or guide you to a quieter spot if needed.
